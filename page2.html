Hobbiton is a meticulously crafted movie set located on a farm near Matamata in the Waikato region of New Zealand, built for Peter Jackson's Lord of the Rings and Hobbit film trilogies. The set features 44 hobbit holes, the famous Party Tree, and the Green Dragon Inn, all nestled into the rolling green hills of the Alexander family farm. Today it operates as a popular tourist destination, offering guided tours that let visitors step into the Shire and experience the magic of Middle-earth firsthand.
